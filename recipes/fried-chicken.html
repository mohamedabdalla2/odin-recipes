Fried Chicken

Description

Make some delicious and finger-lickin golden fried chicken. Extra crunchy on the outside and juice on the inside!

Fried chicken has been described as being "crunchy" and "juicy", as well as "crispy". The dish has also been called "spicy"
and "salty". Occasionally, fried chicken is also topped with chili like paprika, or hot sauce to give it a spicy taste.
