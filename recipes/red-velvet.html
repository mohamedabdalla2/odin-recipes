Red Velvet Cake

Description

Red velvet cake is traditionally a red, crimson, or scarlet corlored layered cake with ermine icing. Traditional recipes did not use food coloring with the red color possible due to non-Dutched, anthrocyanin-rich cocoa and possibly due to the usage of brown sugar, formally called red sugar.Traditionally, red velvet cake is iced with a French-style butter ermine icing (also called roux icing though, while containing butter and flour, it is not made from a roux),[10] which is very light and fluffy, but time-consuming to prepare. Cream cheese frosting and buttercream frosting are variations that have increased in popularity

Ingredients

Icing

  • 1 ½ cups white sugar
  • ½ cup shortening
  • 2 eggs
  • 4 tablespoons red food coloring
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa
